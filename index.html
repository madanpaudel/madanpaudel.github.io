About

Shooting since 2022,
based in Nepal

I picked up a camera in October 2022 and haven't put it down. My work moves between documentary and travel photography — drawn to ritual, landscape, and the quiet dignity of people going about their lives in sacred places.

Much of my work has been made in Nepal and across northern India: the ghats of Vrindavan and Mathura, the rhododendron trails above Pokhara, the crowded streets of Holi, the misty pre-dawn of Pashupatinath.
