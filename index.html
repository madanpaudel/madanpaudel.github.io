Madan Paudel

Faces and places across Nepal and India, told one frame at a time.

Madan Paudel has been making photographs since October 2022 — mostly with a Sony ZV‑E10 and whatever light the street gives him. He moves between candid portraits of strangers and quieter, posed studies of traditional dress, shot across Nepal and India. He's currently preparing a submission for the NIP Annual Photography Competition 2026, and building a Nepali‑language history documentary series for YouTube.
